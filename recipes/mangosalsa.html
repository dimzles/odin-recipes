Avocado Mango Salsa

Description

This salsa is quick and easy to make, plus it's delicious! Once you taste it, you won't be able to stop eating it!

Ingredients

  • Mango
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Red Onion
  • White Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Avocado
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roughly chop all whole ingredients.
  2. Toss together mango, bell pepper, onion, sugar, olive oil, and vinegar in a bowl. Gently fold in diced avocado, and season with salt.
